About Us

Create2Help is a youth-run nonprofit focused on giving back through art. Our mission is “The Ability to Create is the Power to Help.” We offer inclusive spaces through classes, workshops, and projects where individuals can explore their creativity, find joy, and express themselves. By directing artistic talents into charitable efforts, we empower people to become changemakers. We see creativity as a way to promote compassion, connection, and community improvement.

“The ability to create is the power to help.”
